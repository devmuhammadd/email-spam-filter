Tips to Avoid Spam

Check Sender's Email:
Make sure the sender of the email is trustworthy and identifiable.

Spot Grammar Errors:
Email addresses with strange looks or minor misspellings should be avoided, they are most likely spam! Attackers do this to bypass spam filters.

Verify Links:
- To view the full URL of a link, hover over it without clicking. Ensure it corresponds to the official organization's webpage.
- Look for minor typos or alterations in the domain name.

Be Cautious:
- Refrain from inputting private information in pop-up email forms. Legitimate organizations direct you to secure websites.
- Avoid opening suspicious links and files as they may contain malware or viruses.

Keep in mind that phishing tactics evolve over time. Staying informed can greatly reduce the risk of falling victim to such attempts.

Spam Emails

Urgent: Claim your prize within 24 hours!
Congratulations! You've won a luxury vacation package worth $5000. Act now to claim your prize.

Limited Time Offer: 80% discount on all purchases!
Don't miss out on this exclusive offer! Shop now and get massive discounts on all products.

Urgent Action Required: Verify your account immediately!
Your account security is at risk! Click here to verify your account details.

You've been selected as a prize winner!
You've been selected as the lucky winner of a brand new smartphone. Claim it now!

Important Security Update: Immediate action required!
Your account security has been compromised. Take action immediately to secure your account.
